NEW BUT OLD
BY JENNIFER WILLIAMSON


When the

            leaves

                        dance,

              and
A

            new

wind blows;



When the

      birds

      sing,

and
           A new

song

    is

           composed;

        When

deep

            within the

                     rivers

of my

          soul

    I begin to

           hear

        the

                  approaching

sea,

I

              pause

      and

            bask

            in sweet

unity.

    I cannot         bear but to

                  see

            The

                    unshakeable

    connection to

        Everything

around me.
